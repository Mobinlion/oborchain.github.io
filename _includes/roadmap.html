Roadmap 2018

Q1 Q2 Q3 Q4

Q1

The OBORCHAIN platform launches on the Ethereum Mainnet. Users can fund or crowdfund open source issues on GitHub using the OBOR token. Developers can claim the OBOR token after a successful ICO.

Q2

In Quarter 2 we plan to bring our tokens to a number of exchanges online.

Q3

Upon successful exchange addition of our token we plan to launch our blockchain network and release it to the public.

Q4

Crowdsale investors now reap the benefit of investing in our large array of projects and hence are rewarded tokens for 0.5% of every 10k tokens owned.
